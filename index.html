...And what's this all about?

If you can't tell by now, I'm Mike Kuzin, an interactive graphics and web designer & developer with a B.S. from the Art Institute of Pittsburgh in Graphic Design. I specilize in clean semenatic web layouts with a focus on web standards and usability.

With my background & schooling in design, as well as my self-taught coding skills, I am able to tackle any project. From logo design & multi-page print publications, to high-end user interface design & content management templating.

If you like what you see, and would like to discuss a project, have a question, or just an idea: drop me a line.
